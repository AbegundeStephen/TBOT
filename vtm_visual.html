📋 Which Setting Should You Use?

🥇 For GOLD Trading

High win rate (84%), tight moves (2-5%)

enable_early_profit_lock=True early_lock_threshold_pct=0.01

Lock at 1% gain. Protects your winners without cutting them short. Gold rarely has huge reversals.

🥈 For BTC Position Trading

Lower frequency, big swings (10-50%)

enable_early_profit_lock=False

Let it breathe. Lock after first partial (+12%). BTC needs room for volatility. Catches the 50%+ moves.

🥉 For BTC Day Trading

Medium frequency, 5-15% targets

enable_early_profit_lock=True early_lock_threshold_pct=0.02

Lock at 2% gain. Balances protection with profit potential. Good for overnight holds.

💡 Key Insight

Early profit locking is like insurance: You pay a small premium (potentially smaller gains) to protect against catastrophic losses. For high-frequency Gold trading, it's worth it. For low-frequency BTC position trading, you can self-insure with wide stops.

The sweet spot: Lock early enough to protect capital, but not so early that you kill every winner before it develops. 1% for GOLD, 2% for BTC day trading, no early lock for BTC position trading.
