🎯 Signal Aggregator Systems

Understanding the Institutional Council vs Performance-Weighted Approaches

📊 System Overview

Two Complementary Approaches to Signal Generation

Your trading system employs two distinct aggregation methodologies, each optimized for different market conditions:

  • Institutional Council: A rule-based, judge-scored system mimicking institutional decision-making
  • Performance-Weighted: A statistical, confidence-weighted system optimized for complex markets
  • Hybrid Selector: Automatically switches between the two based on market regime analysis

🏛️ Institutional Council Aggregator

🏛️
Institutional Council
"BlackRock-Style" Weighted Voting System

Core Philosophy: Mimics how institutional traders make decisions - multiple independent "judges" evaluate the market, each with specific expertise and voting power.

1. TREND Judge 1.5 pts
The Boss: Evaluates EMA alignment and overall market direction
Scoring:
• Price > EMA50 AND EMA20 > EMA50 = Full credit (1.5 pts)
• Price > EMA50 BUT EMA20 < EMA50 = Partial (0.75 pts)
• Price below EMA50 = No credit (0 pts)
2. STRUCTURE Judge 1.5 pts
The Location Expert: Validates price position relative to S/R levels
Scoring:
• At key S/R level (AI + indicators) = Full credit (1.5 pts)
• Near S/R (within threshold) = Partial (0.75 pts)
• No S/R nearby = No credit (0 pts)
3. MOMENTUM Judge 1.0 pt
The Fuel Gauge: Measures RSI and MACD strength
Scoring:
• RSI in bullish zone (40-65) = Full credit (1.0 pt)
• RSI oversold (<30) = Dip-buying bonus (1.0 pt)
• MACD confirmation = +0.2 bonus
4. PATTERN Judge 0.5 pt
The Trigger: Validates AI candlestick patterns
Scoring:
• AI pattern confidence > 75% = Full credit (0.5 pt)
• AI pattern confidence > 60% = Partial (0.4 pt)
• Below threshold = No credit (0 pts)
5. VOLUME Judge 0.5 pt
The Validator: Confirms volume strength
Scoring:
• Volume > 1.5x MA(20) = Full credit (0.5 pt)
• Volume > MA(20) = Partial (0.35 pt)
• Below average = No credit (0 pts)

Decision Process

1
Detect Market Regime
EMA strategy identifies if market is BULL or BEAR
2
Each Judge Evaluates
All 5 judges independently score the market setup (0 to max points)
3
Calculate Total Score
Sum all judge scores (max possible: 5.0 points)
4
Apply Threshold Rules
• Trend-aligned: Need ≥ 3.5 / 5.0 (70% approval)
• Counter-trend: Need ≥ 4.0 / 5.0 (80% unanimous)
5
AI Validation (Optional)
If enabled, AI can veto signals that lack pattern/S/R confirmation

Council Score Requirements

Trend-Aligned Trade (e.g., BUY in BULL market):

3.5 pts ✓

Simple majority required (70%)

Counter-Trend Trade (e.g., BUY in BEAR market):

4.0 pts ✓

Near-unanimous required (80%)

📊 Performance-Weighted Aggregator

📊
Performance-Weighted
Statistical Confidence-Based Aggregation

Core Philosophy: Treats each strategy as a weighted contributor. Signals are aggregated based on confidence levels, agreement patterns, and regime context.

📈 Strategy Composition

The aggregator combines three strategies with equal base weights:

Mean Reversion (50%) - DECISION MAKER

50%

Identifies oversold/overbought conditions, pivot bounces

Trend Following (50%) - DECISION MAKER

50%

Captures momentum breakouts, trend continuations

EMA Strategy - REGIME DETECTOR

REGIME ONLY

Does NOT contribute to signal, only identifies BULL/BEAR regime

Decision Process

1
Detect Regime (EMA Strategy)
Multi-factor analysis: EMA alignment, momentum (20d/50d), volatility, ADX, MACD, RSI
2
Get Strategy Signals
Mean Reversion and Trend Following each return: signal (-1/0/1) + confidence (0-1)
3
AI Validation (Optional)
Each non-zero signal validated against:
• Pattern recognition (Sniper AI)
• S/R proximity analysis
Rejected signals → set to 0 with 0 confidence
4
Calculate Buy & Sell Scores
For each direction, sum contributions:
• Agreement: +full confidence × weight
• Hold: +partial confidence × weight × 20%
• Opposition: -confidence × weight × 50%
5
Apply Bonuses & Regime Adjustments
• 2-strategy agreement: +0.25 bonus
• Bull regime: +0.25 for BUY, -0.20 for SELL
• Bear regime: -0.30 for BUY, +0.25 for SELL
6
Dynamic Threshold Adjustment
Thresholds adapt based on regime confidence:
• Bull: Lower buy threshold, higher sell threshold
• Bear: Higher buy threshold, lower sell threshold
7
Make Final Decision
Compare scores to thresholds:
• Buy score ≥ threshold AND > sell score → BUY
• Sell score ≥ threshold AND > buy score → SELL
• Otherwise → HOLD
8
Signal Quality Filter
Calculate signal_quality (0-1) from:
• Base score (capped at 0.7)
• Agreement penalty if no consensus
• Regime alignment bonus
Reject if quality < min_threshold (0.28 BTC / 0.25 GOLD)

🔒 AI Circuit Breaker (Safety Feature)

Prevents AI from over-filtering signals:

  • Tracks last 50 AI validation decisions
  • If rejection rate > 85%, circuit breaker triggers
  • Temporarily disables AI validation for 10 signals
  • Prevents "death by AI over-filtering"

🔄 Hybrid Aggregator Selector

🔄
Intelligent Mode Switching
Automatically selects the best aggregator for current market conditions

Core Concept: Instead of picking one aggregator and sticking with it, the Hybrid Selector analyzes real-time market structure and dynamically switches between Council and Performance modes.

Selection Process

1
Market Regime Analysis
The MarketRegimeAnalyzer calculates 6 key metrics:
• Trend Strength: ADX + Directional Movement (strong/weak)
• Volatility Regime: ATR ratio (high/normal/low)
• Price Clarity: Indecision candle % (clear/mixed/noisy)
• Momentum Alignment: RSI + MACD agreement
• S/R Proximity: Distance to key levels
• Regime Classification: trending_clean, ranging_quiet, etc.
2
Score Both Aggregators
Each aggregator gets a score (0-9 for Council, 0-10 for Performance) based on market fit
3
Apply Decision Rules
• Winner = highest score
• Confidence = score / max_possible_score
• Tie-breaker: Use regime type
4
Check Cooldown & Confidence
• Only switch if confidence ≥ 65%
• Minimum 30 minutes between switches
• Prevents excessive mode flipping
5
Execute Switch & Notify
• Update current mode
• Log detailed reasoning
• Send Telegram notification
• Track statistics
