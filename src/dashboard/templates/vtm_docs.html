🔄 What's Changed in Current VTM

1. Structure-Based Stop Loss (Wider & Safer)

OLD: Used min(atr_stop, structure_stop) → chose the TIGHTER stop
NEW: Uses max(atr_stop, structure_stop) → chooses the WIDER stop
Result: BTC stops are now ~0.5-1.0% instead of 0.2-0.3%, giving trades more breathing room

2. Hybrid Take-Profit Targets

NEW: TPs now blend market structure with R:R multiples
• Detects resistance levels (longs) or support levels (shorts)
• Places targets AT structure if within 20% of R:R target
• Falls back to R:R if no structure exists
Result: Higher probability exits at natural turning points

3. More Realistic Target Multiples

OLD: BTC partials at [1.5R, 2.5R, 4.0R]
NEW: BTC partials at [1.5R, 2.5R, 3.5R] (more achievable)
Result: Better chance of hitting all partials before reversal

4. Enhanced Safety Checks

• Minimum 1.5 ATR distance enforced for stops (prevents ultra-tight stops)
• Position size validation (prevents >2x account balance positions)
• Better logging for stop calculations

📋 Which Setting Should You Use?

🥇 For GOLD Trading

Best for: High win rate (80-85%), tight moves (0.5-2%), frequent trades

enable_early_profit_lock=True early_lock_threshold_pct=0.01

Why: Gold moves are smaller and reversals are common. Locking at 1% protects your 80%+ win rate without cutting winners short. The structure-based TPs (usually 0.5-1.5%) align perfectly with Gold's typical ranges.

🥈 For BTC Position Trading

Best for: Lower frequency, big swings (5-30%), holding 1-5 days

enable_early_profit_lock=False

Why: BTC position trades need breathing room. The wider structure-based stops (0.5-1.0%) give trades space. Let it run to first partial (1.5R) before locking. Catches the 10-50%+ moves without premature exits.

🥉 For BTC Swing Trading

Best for: Medium frequency, 2-8% targets, holding 4-24 hours

enable_early_profit_lock=True early_lock_threshold_pct=0.015

Why: Lock at 1.5% gain. Balances protection with profit potential. Good for overnight holds where you're not actively monitoring. The hybrid TPs find natural resistance points.

💡 Key Insights with Updated VTM

1. Structure-Based Stops Are Safer: The new logic uses max(atr_stop, structure_stop), giving you ~0.5-1.0% stops on BTC instead of the old 0.2-0.3%. This prevents premature stop-outs from normal market noise.

2. Hybrid TPs Improve Win Rate: By placing targets AT market structure (resistance/support), you're exiting where price is likely to stall anyway. Falls back to R:R when no structure exists.

3. Early Locking Still Valuable: Even with wider stops, early profit locking at 1% (Gold) or 1.5% (BTC) protects against reversals. It's insurance that costs almost nothing when trades go your way.

The Formula: Gold = Lock early (1%) + Tight natural stops (0.2-0.3%) | BTC Position = No early lock + Wide structure stops (0.5-1.0%) | BTC Swing = Lock at 1.5% + Medium stops (0.4-0.6%)

🔧 How The New Logic Works Together

Stop Loss: Finds pivot structure → Calculates ATR-based stop → Takes the WIDER one → Enforces minimum 1.5 ATR distance → Result: Safer, less noise

Take Profits: Detects resistance/support levels → Calculates R:R targets (1.5R, 2.5R, 3.5R) → Blends them (structure if within 20%) → Result: Higher probability exits

Early Lock: Optional safety net that moves stop to break-even at your chosen % gain → Doesn't interfere with targets → Only prevents big losses → Result: Sleep better at night
